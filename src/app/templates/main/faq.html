Why is the real-time sensor value (in the gauge) the same as the one logged to the database(in the graph)?

Real-time sensor values are generated every minute while the logged sensors value are computed every 10 (for environmental sensors) or 15 (for plant sensors) minutes. The logged value correspond to the median of the last three measures read by the sensors. This allow to remove incorrect sensor reads and to smoothen the data.
